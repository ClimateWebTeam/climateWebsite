Whether you’re seeking great food and drink, a swim and sauna, or Afternoon Tea after visiting the museums and shops of The Loch Inn offers it all. Free full English breakfast and the stunning Palette Restaurant are among the hotel’s most popular features, along with discounted access to the adjacent spa, Aquilla Health and Fitness. Friendly, 24-hour staff – including many Golden Keys concierges – go out of their way to make your stay in London perfect. They’ll arrange tours, tickets, restaurant bookings, chauffeurs and taxis. Gym is available for the use of members and contains a comprehensive variety of free weights, fixed-weight machines and specialist, paddling-specific equipment. Adjacent health club and spa, with a heated indoor swimming pool, sauna, jacuzzi, steam bath and fully equipped gym.Discounted day rate of £10 for guests (free with some accommodation packages) Massages and beauty treatments available Food is available in the mainland clubhouse everyday and Saturday mornings after training. Wednesdays are the main Loch Inn nights.
