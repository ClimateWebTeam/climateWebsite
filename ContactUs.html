Contact Us

You Can Contact Us In A Variety Of Ways:

  • For complaints, comments and copyright information – Please read our guidance or send us an email.
  • For speaking and event invitations or to contact the Chief Executive, please email private.secretary@thecmp.org.uk. If your request is urgent, please call +44 (0) 7985 206951 during working hours.
  • For press enquiries only, please call +44 (0)20 8026 7793 / +44 (0)20 8720 2368 during working hours or +44(0) 7940 703911 / +44 (0) 7766 366577 out of hours. Please do not call these numbers unless you are a member of the news media.
  • Alternatively, for general enquiries please send us a message, using the form below. We monitor enquiries throughout the working day (Monday to Friday, 9am-5.00pm) and aim to provide a response as promptly as we can.
